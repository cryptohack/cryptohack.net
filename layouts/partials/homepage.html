The internet is an amazing tool for freedom of information, communication and education. But it's under attack from corporate interests and dragnet government surveillance.

There is hope! We have the tools to help everyone take control of their privacy. More and more people are encrypting their communications, but many of the tools are still hard to use.

Hackers for Privacy have the technical skills (UX, testing, security, development, etc) to improve the tools so everyone can benefit from them.

Hackers for Privacy have the will to stand up and defend our digital privacy.
